About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in Statistics at the Department of Economics, Management and Statistics (DEMS) at University of Milano-Bicocca (Milan, Italy), working under the supervision of Prof. Bernardo Nipoti (DEMS, University of Milano-Bicocca).

I have received my PhD degree in Statistics at Universiy of Padova (Padova, Italy) in 2022 under the supervision of Prof. Antonio Canale (Department of Statistical Sciences, University of Padova) and Prof. Michele Guindani (Department of Biostatistics, UCLA).
