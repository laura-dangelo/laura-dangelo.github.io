About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in Statistics at the Department of Economics, Management and Statistics (DEMS) at the University of Milano-Bicocca (Milan, Italy), working under the supervision of Prof. Bernardo Nipoti (DEMS, University of Milano-Bicocca).
I received my PhD in Statistics at the Universiy of Padova (Padova, Italy) in 2022, supervised by Prof. Antonio Canale (Department of Statistical Sciences, University of Padova) and Prof. Michele Guindani (Department of Biostatistics, UCLA).

Interests

  • Bayesian modeling
  • Model-based clustering
  • Bayesian nonparametrics
  • Computational statistics
